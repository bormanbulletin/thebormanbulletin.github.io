Profile

Me & My Experience

I'm running for Sophomore Class President not because I have all the answers, but because I believe the best ideas come from listening. Leadership is service — and service starts with understanding the people you represent.

I've seen what happens when leaders decide in isolation. I'm committed to doing it differently. Coming to Campo from Stanley Middle School taught me that community is built intentionally — and that diversity of perspective is a strength I want to bring to this role.

Platform

The Mission

A presidency isn't about one person's ideas — it's about turning your ideas into action. Here's the commitment.

01

A Culture of Listening

Monthly open forums, an anonymous feedback system, and a commitment to logging every concern. Every suggestion gets considered.

02

Events That Include Everyone

Events planned through student surveys and input — not decided by a small group. The best events feel like they were made for us, because they were.

03

Transparency & Accountability

Public goals, public results. No more guessing what your class president actually does — you'll know, because I'll tell you.

04

Bridging the Gap

A real liaison between students and administration — your ideas go directly to the people who can act on them.

05

Representing Every Student

My goal is to actively seek out the voices that are often overlooked — no group at Campolindo should feel invisible.
